Caidas de distinto nivel

Medidas de control

(*)Sólo transite por senderos habilitados

(*)Respete limites de acercamiento a acantilados

(*)No corra o haga bromas en sectores, como acantilados, o de superficies irregulares

(*)Se recomienda uso de calzado que sujete tobillo y tenga un buen dibujo de suela

Fall at level and different level

Measures of control

(*)Transit along authorized footpaths only.

(*)Respect approach cliffs limits.

(*)Do not run nor do pranks in areas, like cliffs, or irregular surfaces.

(*)Use of footwear that holds the ankle and has a good sole pattern is recommended

Factores climaticos adversos

Medidas de control

(*)Cumplir con Protocolo de emergencia del Valle de la Luna

(*)En caso de lluvias, rayos estar atentos a instrucción de administración del Valle para generar evacuación, mantenga la calma y evacue a puntos de resguardo

(*)Vientos por sobre 70 KMS /Hrs, evacuación de zonas de riesgo

(*)Respetar cierre temporal de Valle de la Luna, o sectores específicos afectados

LOS OPÉRADORES TURISTICOS SERAN RESPONSABLES DE LA SEGURIDAD DE SUS PASAJEROS Y DEBERAN ACATAR Y DAR SOPORTE A LAS POSIBLES EVACUACIONES DEL VALLE DE LA LUNA POR FACTORES CLIMATICOS ADVERSOS.

Adverse weather factors

Measures of control

(*)Comply with The Moon Valley’s emergency protocol.

(*)In case of rain, rays, be attentive of administration of the valley’s instructions for evacuation, keep calm and evacuate to shelter points.

(*)Winds over 70 KMS/Hrs, Evacuation of risk areas. 

(*)Respect temporary closure of the Moon Valley, or specific affected areas.

THE TOUR OPERATORS WILL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR THE SECURITY OF ITS PASSENGERS AND SHOULD ABIDE AND GIVE SUPPORT TO POSSIBLE EVACUATIONS BY CLIMATIC FACTORS OF THE MOON VALLEY.

(Propios Obligatorios, recomendacion para turistas)

(*)Uso de ropa cuello redondo, mangas

Exposures to UV radiation

Measures of control

(*)Use photo protector FPS 50+

(own obligatory, recommendation for tourists)

(*)Wear round neck clothes, long sleeves and long trousers 

(*)Camping hat type and/or with sun visors and protection to the back of the neck.

(*)Use of glasses with UV protection

(Own obligatory, recommendation for tourists).

Perdida de control del vehiculo

Medidas de control

(*)Respetar velocidad al interior del Valle 20 KM/Hrs

(*)Vehículos con mantención al día

(*)Al transitar cercano a personas o ciclistas mantenga distancia y baje velocidad.

(*)Transitar por rutas habilitadas y autorizadas

(*)Dar fiel cumplimiento a ley 18,290

Loss of control of vehicles

Control measures

(*)Respect speed inside the Valley 20 KM/Hrs.

(*)Vehicles with daily maintenance.

(*)Keep distance when driving if you’re close from people or bikers.

(*)Transit through open and authorized routes.

(*)Safeguarding compliance of the law 18,290.

Perdida de control sobre bicicleta

Medidas de control

(*)Antes de ingresar al valle verificar correcto estado de la bicicleta, (frenos, ruedas, asegurar manubrio etc).

(*)Uso de casco y chaleco reflectante

(*)Varios ciclistas transitar uno en pos de otro

(*)Respetar protocolos de seguridad Valle de la Luna, en caso de emergencia

(*)Se prohíbe tomarse de otros vehículos que se encuentren en movimiento (Ley 18.290 Art,131)

(*)Se prohíbe transportar carga, que impida mantener ambas manos en el manubrio (Ley 18.290 Art,132)

(*)En caso de poca iluminación transitar uno en pos del otro (Ley 18.290 Art,131)

Loss of control on bicycle

Measures of control

(*)Before entering the Valley check bicycle conditions (brakes, wheels, securing handlebars, etc).

(*)Use of helmet and reflective vest.

(*)Several cyclists transit one toward another.

(*)Respect The Moon Valley’s safety protocols in case of emergency.

(*)It is prohibited to take other vehicles that are in motion

(Law 18. 290 Art,131).

(*)It is prohibited to carry loads, which prevents both hands from holding on the handlebars

(Act 18. 290 Art,132).

(*)In case of low light transit one toward another

(Law 18. 290 Art,131).
